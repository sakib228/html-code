A paragraph is a distinct section of a written text that focuses on a single idea, consisting of one or more sentences, and is typically set off by an indentation or a new line.

Paragraphs are used to organize writing into manageable, easy-to-read sections, making it easier for readers to follow the author's thoughts and the overall structure of the piece.

They achieve this by introducing new topics or subtopics, developing a single controlling idea, and helping to transition between different points.
